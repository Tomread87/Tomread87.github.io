Spaghetti Bolognese

The Bolognese sauce is prepared with few ingredients, the steps are simple, it only needs to cook slowly for a few hours. The base of this dressing is a fried vegetables and bacon to which it is added to the minced meat and the tomato puree. The result is a very good sauce suitable for any type of pasta.

Ingredients

  • 600 g Minced meat
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 celery stalk
  • 100 g Bacon
  • 1\2 glass White wine
  • 70 ml Extra virgin olive Oil
  • 350 g Tomato puree
  • Salt

Let's start Cooking!

Cut the bacon into small cubes Prepare a chopped carrot, onion and celery Pour the oil into the pan and fry the bacon

Then pour the mixture of vegetables cut into small pieces When the vegetables are wilted add the minced meat and cook for a few minutes over high heat

Add half a glass of white wine and let it evaporate while mixing the ingredients When the wine has evaporated, add the tomato puree and cook over low heat for about 2 hours. Salt the ragù
